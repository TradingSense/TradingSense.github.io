What is TradingSense?

TradingSense provides trading signals and stock market information to investors. Using machine learning, we forecast the price of the most popular stocks and backtest these forecasts to ensure we are providing the most accurate information to investors. Money flow index and williams percent range are incorporated into our machine learning software to determine the best trading move for investors. Our backtests indicate our forecasts are on average, 90% accurate.

---

TradingSense began as a twitter bot that automatically tracked the price fluctuations of top stocks. At first, even support of stocks was limited. Eventually, all stocks became supported and trackable. The idea of "Predicting" stock prices became intruiging and brought on the idea of using Machine Learning to recognize and forecast the trend patterns of stocks using years of past data. Due to the volatility of the stock market, it is impossible to predict its exaxt movements. However, we were able to predict the prices of stocks with 80-95% accuracy on a regular basis. The accuracy of a prediction can also be forecasted, by backtesting predictions with the real price outcome an average accruacy can be given to give users a sense of how accurate the most recent prediction is.

Signals

Buy - Signaled when stock is considered to be at a low according to Williams Range, MFI is approaching overvalued state, and LTSM forecasts stock to increase in price

Sell - Signaled when stock is at a high according to Williams Range, MfI is approaching undervalued state, and LTSM forecasts decrease in price.

Hold - Signaled when stock is considered higher than normal according to Williams Range and LTSM forecasts a significant increase OR any stagnant condition
