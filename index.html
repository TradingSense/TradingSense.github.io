Sense The Market With Machine Learning

TradingSense provides trading signals and stock market information to investors. Using machine learning, we forecast the price of the most popular stocks like tesla and amazon then backtest these forecasts to ensure we are providing the most accurate information to investors. Money flow index and williams percent range are incorporated into our machine learning software to determine the best trading move for investors. Our backtests indicate our forecasts are on average, 90% accurate. Our daily stock predictions are perfect for anyone interesting in the outlook of their investments.

Programmed using python, our software is lightweight and is constantly undergoing updates to improve accuracy and functionality. Users experiencing any issues should email tradingsenseapp@gmail.com
