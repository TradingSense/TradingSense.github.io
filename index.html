What is TradingSense?

TradingSense provides trading signals and stock information to investors. Using machine learning, we forecast the price of the most popular stocks and backtest these forecasts to ensure we are providing the most accurate information to investors. Money flow index and williams percent range are incorporated into our machine learning software to determine the best trading move for investors. Our backtests indicate our forecasts are on average, 90% accurate.
